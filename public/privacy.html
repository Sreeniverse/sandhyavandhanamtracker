Privacy Policy

Sandhyavandhanam Tracker - Last updated: June 10, 2026

Sandhyavandhanam Tracker ("the App") is provided by Asthika Samaj. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, and optional phone number, used to create and manage your account.
  • Family member names: if you choose to track practice for family members, the names you enter for them.
  • Practice records: your daily Sandhyavandhanam tracking entries (morning, afternoon, evening completion status and timestamps).
  • Notification data: your reminder preferences, timezone, and a push notification token (if you enable reminders) so we can deliver scheduled reminders.
  • Technical and error logs: limited diagnostic information (error messages, device/browser type) used to fix bugs. These logs do not include your practice records.

How We Use Information

  • To provide core app functionality - tracking your practice and showing your history.
  • To send you optional reminder notifications at the times you choose.
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues.

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Supabase, a third-party database and authentication provider, with access controls (row-level security) ensuring you can only access your own data and that of family members you manage. Data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.

Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile page within the app. Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, practice records, family member records, and notification data from our systems.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental involvement (e.g., a parent tracking practice on behalf of a family member).

Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your personal information at any time through the app's Profile page, or by contacting us using the details below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
