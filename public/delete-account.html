Sandhyavandhanam Tracker - Asthika Samaj

How to delete your account from the app

  1. Open Sandhyavandhanam Tracker and sign in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Account & Data".
  4. Type your email address to confirm, then tap Delete.

This action is immediate and cannot be undone.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes:

  • Your profile (name, email, phone number)
  • All practice records (activity history) for your account and any family members you added
  • Family member records you created
  • Notification preferences and push notification subscriptions

No data is retained after deletion, and there is no additional retention period.

Can't access the app?

If you're unable to sign in or use the app, email huntersreenihs@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account, and we will delete your account and data manually within a reasonable time.
